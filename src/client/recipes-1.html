Fried Egg Plant
Edit

By Kurt

Recipe

  • Title: Fried Egg Plant
    • Serves: 4
    • Prep Time: 5 mins
    • Total Time: 30 mins
Ingredients:
  • One Large Egg Plant
  • 2 Eggs
  • Italian Style Bread Crumbs
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  • Slice eggplant from top to bottom (on its side). Cut about 1/8 in thickness.
  • Heat frying pan on medium. Pour in about a tablespoon or more of olive oil to cover pan.
  • Wisk two eggs in bowl.
  • Sprinkle bread crumbs on plate.
  • Dip each sliced eggplant into egg batter and cover with bread crumbs on each side.
  • Fill frying plan with battered egg plant slices.
  • Flip eggplant when golden brown.
  • Let cool on plate with paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  • Enjoy solo or with a main meal.
